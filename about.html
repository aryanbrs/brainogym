The Heart of Brainogym: A Founder's Inspiration

In 2008, Mrs. Kavita Prasoon, a thriving professional who had stepped away from her corporate career to embrace motherhood, stumbled upon the world of abacus and mental math. Fascinated by its ability to sharpen young minds, what started as a way to help her own child became a transformative personal journey.

With no business background but unlimited belief, she started small: a single classroom in Laxmi Nagar, Delhi. Word spread as parents saw real progress — their children became quicker, sharper, and more confident. Her passion project blossomed into a movement.

Today, BRAINOGYM Educare stands tall with a presence in over 50 schools and 25 dedicated centres across India, touching thousands of young lives. It's a testament to every parent who believed in alternative learning and every child who discovered they were capable of more.
