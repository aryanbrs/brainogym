# Homepage Copy Framework (Conversion-First)

## Brand Positioning
- BrainoGym is a child development institute focused on cognitive skills, confidence, and future-readiness.
- Tone: expert, warm, trustworthy, measurable outcomes.

## Hero Copy (Primary Recommendation)
- Eyebrow: `Since 2008 | Child Skill Development Experts`
- Headline: `Build Faster Thinking, Stronger Confidence, and Better Academic Outcomes`
- Subheadline: `Abacus, Vedic Maths, Robotics, and Personality Development programs designed for real growth.`
- Primary CTA: `Book Free Demo Class`
- Secondary CTA: `Talk to Counselor`
- Trust strip: `10,000+ learners | 50+ school associations | Parent-trusted programs`

## Alternative Hero Headlines (A/B test ready)
1. `Turn Potential Into Performance With BrainoGym`
2. `Where Kids Learn to Think Faster, Focus Better, and Lead Confidently`
3. `Future-Ready Skills for Children Through Proven Learning Programs`

## Metrics Block Copy
- Section title: `Real Learning. Real Outcomes.`
- Cards:
  - `Years of Excellence`
  - `Students Trained`
  - `School Collaborations`
  - `Centers / Batches`
- Note: replace all placeholders (`0+`) with verified data before launch.

## Programs Block Copy
- Section title: `Programs Designed for Complete Development`
- Intro: `Each program is structured to improve cognitive speed, confidence, and practical problem-solving.`
- Program CTA label: `Explore Program`

## Trust/Social Proof Block Copy
- Section title: `Parents Trust BrainoGym for Measurable Progress`
- Content types:
  - Parent testimonial snippets
  - Student transformation highlights
  - Result/proof image references
- CTA: `View Results & Success Stories`

## Founder Block Copy
- Section title: `A Parent-Led Vision, Built for Every Child’s Growth`
- Summary: short founder mission, teaching philosophy, and legacy trust statement.
- CTA: `Read Founder Story`

## Why BrainoGym Block Copy
- Section title: `Why Families Choose BrainoGym`
- Bullet themes:
  - Structured curriculum
  - Expert trainers
  - Confidence-first approach
  - Performance tracking
  - Supportive learning ecosystem

## FAQ Mini-Section (High Intent)
- Sample questions:
  - `Which age groups can join?`
  - `How soon can I see progress?`
  - `Do you offer trial classes?`
  - `Are online classes available?`

## Final CTA Band
- Headline: `Ready to Start Your Child’s Growth Journey?`
- Subheadline: `Book a free demo and get personalized program guidance.`
- Primary CTA: `Book Free Demo Class`
- Secondary CTA: `Call Now`

## Sticky Conversion Elements
- Mobile sticky button: `Book Demo`
- WhatsApp quick connect (optional): `Chat With Counselor`

## SEO Copy Notes
- Include local relevance naturally (Delhi location and service areas).
- Use parent search intent terms in headings and body copy.
- Keep paragraphs short and scannable for mobile users.
